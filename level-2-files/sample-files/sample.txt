Duke University - Quick Facts
==============================

Founded: 1838 (as Brown's Schoolhouse), renamed Trinity College in 1859,
         became Duke University in 1924.

Location: Durham, North Carolina, USA.

Motto: "Eruditio et Religio" (Knowledge and Faith).

Campus: 8,610 acres, including the Duke Forest -- one of the largest
        university-maintained forests in the United States.

Chapel: The Duke Chapel stands 210 feet tall and is built in the
        Collegiate Gothic style. It took four years to build (1930-1935).

Colors: Duke Blue and White.

Mascot: The Blue Devil, inspired by the French "les Diables Bleus"
        (the Blue Devils), an elite Alpine military unit from World War I.

Fun fact: Duke's men's basketball program has won five NCAA championships.
Coach Mike Krzyzewski ("Coach K") retired in 2022 as the winningest
coach in Division I men's basketball history, with 1,202 career wins.
